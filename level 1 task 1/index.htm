Sri Vasavi Engineering College

Sri Vasavi Engineering College, considered one of the best engineering colleges in Andhra Pradesh, started its journey in the year 2001. Since its inception, the college has been striving to provide quality technical education to its students and making them industry ready. Sri Vasavi Engineering College exemplifies quality in various fields ranging from Engineering, Pharmacy to Business Administration courses and is rated as one among the top 10 engineering colleges in Andhra Pradesh.
